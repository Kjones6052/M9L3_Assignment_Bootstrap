Objective: The aim of this assignment is to familiarize students with Bootstrap's typography and color classes and their application in web design.

Problem Statement:

understand how Bootstrap's typography classes can style text elements effectively and how its color palette can be utilized to create visually 
appealing designs. Note: You can enhance any of the pages created on previous assignments or create a new one, it's up to you!
